Non-Profit Membership

Designed exclusively for non-profit organizations, this membership provides:

  • Community Connections: Networking opportunities to collaborate with local businesses and other non-profits.
  • Basic Directory Listing: Inclusion in the Chamber’s online business directory to enhance visibility.
  • Event Invitations: Access to Chamber events at discounted rates to promote your mission.
  • Recognition: Highlighted as a valued non-profit partner in our community outreach.
Bronze Membership

Perfect for small businesses looking to grow and connect, the Bronze level offers:

  • Directory Listing: Priority placement in the Chamber’s business directory.
  • Networking Events: Invitations to Chamber mixers and events to expand your professional network.
  • Social Media Shoutouts: One promotional post on the Chamber’s social media platforms annually.
  • Educational Resources: Access to webinars and workshops focused on small business growth.
Silver Memberhsip

Ideal for businesses seeking elevated exposure and engagement, the Silver level includes:

  • Enhanced Directory Listing: Featured placement with a business logo and detailed profile in the Chamber directory.
  • Event Discounts: Reduced pricing for booth space at Chamber-hosted events and expos.
  • Advertising Opportunities: One free ad in the Chamber’s newsletter and website per year.
  • Exclusive Invitations: Invitations to Silver and above networking events, including leadership roundtables.
Gold Memberhsip

For businesses aiming for maximum visibility and comunity impact, the Gold level offers:

  • Premium Directory Listing: Top-tier placement in the business directory with a dedicated landing page.
  • Leadership Opportunities: Eligibility to serve on Chamber committees or advisory boards.
  • Unlimited Promotion: Ongoing promotion on the Chamber’s website, social media, and newsletters.
  • Event Sponsorships: Priority sponsorship opportunities for Chamber events to showcase your brand.
  • Complimentary Tickets: Free tickets to select Chamber-hosted events and galas.
